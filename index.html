Some information about us

Established in 2014, and our directors has over 10 years of experience in agribusiness and distribution with competitive pricing the company launched small ranges in household and DIY and brands for the general consumer market ( Retailers ) .

Company started with sourcing Pigeon Peas and Nuts. Through expanding and working with small traders in India and Africa and maintaining good reputation in the industry.

We were able to expand our list of products to accommodate various types of Oil Seeds, Pulses and Beans and over the years company diversified supplying quality consumer products in the UK and considering expending in North America and in Africa.

About Us

GCP Group has established an excellent supply chain which focuses on procuring quality products from various international companies such as UK / US Greeting cards, Duracell, Panasonic, Prym . Our products are constantly and closely monitored by our experienced and trained staff; this ensures high specifications and maximum valuable product delivered to our customers.

We are able to offer our products to our customers at a very competitive market price. We take extra care to ensure that the products meet the highest quality standards that our buyers expect. We believe that long term business relationship is built on trust and mutual understanding which in turn creates company’s success and satisfied customers.

who we are

GCP Group has established a well-managed supply chain which focuses on procuring quality consumer products from local and international market. Cleaning and packaging of the products are constantly monitored and maintained by experience colleague, so that the products can reach high standards and maximum value can be delivered to our customers.

Satisfying the customer’s choice is very important for us, so if there are any desirable preference in the quality and the packing of the products, we cater accordingly.

Through in-house logistics and maintaining good relationship with quality service providers, we can meet our shipments on time according to the customer’s needs. Quality standards are maintained at all levels along the supply chain, for all commodities, hence enabling us deliver quality products to our customers.
